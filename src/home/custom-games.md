Blind Picture Guess
Set up:

Two teams. One game master.
Two pieces of cloths to cover the eyes of two players.
Paper and pen for each team
A list of words to guess
Game plays in multiple rounds. The winner of the most rounds wins.

At beginning of a round, the game master tells in secret a player in each team one of the word to guess. These players must be as different as possible each round.

The two players with the secret word are then moved in front of the paper for their team. The two players are given the pen and blind folded.

The game master gives a go signal. The blind folded players must draw on the paper to get their team to guess the secret word. They cannot do anything else.

The first team to guess the current secret word wins the round. After one minute, the game master can stop a round calling it a draw result.

The words must be chosen carefully to not be too easy or too hard to draw. They can be short expressions. Examples of words: cellular phone, pineapple, piano, or summer vacation.

Song Acting
Set up:

Two teams and at least one judge.
Loud music system with universal music service like Spotify
Room large enough for two teams to act a song at the same time
Optional list of songs to act
Game plays in multiple rounds. The winner of the most rounds wins.

At beginning of each round the teams have a few minutes to pick a song and design an interesting act on the song. Acting can be a choreography, lip sync, air guitar, or whatever will impress the judge.

Each team act their selected song one at a time. Judge can stop the music when the energy become lower.

Optional: The judge can pick a song instead of letting the teams. The team have a few minutes to prepare then must act at the same time.

Shooter Creation Contest
Set up:

Two teams and at least one judge.
Many shooter building bottles and food elements
At least six shooter glasses
Each team has a few minutes to prepare 3 identical shooters. Both teams act at the same time.

After the time limit is reached, the judge assess the presentation and tastes each team shooter.

The loosing team must drink their shooters. The winning team can decide to drink its shooters or have the loosing team drink them.

Candy Catch
Set up:

Two teams of at least two people each
Small throw-able candies
Game plays in multiple rounds. The winner of the most rounds wins.

Players from each team must throw a candy at an other player at least 6 feet away. The receiving player must catch the candy in his mouth and eat it. Once the candy is in the air he can move as he wants.

Teams score one point when they catch the candy.

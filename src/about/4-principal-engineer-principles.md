# 4 Principal Engineer Principles

**Tell me about your ability to deliver results on time, to ensure delivery progress is on track. What process did you put in place to ensure this**

## Technical Dive Deep

## Quantitative Decision Making

**Tell me about a time where you used metrics and measures to improve your decisions**
